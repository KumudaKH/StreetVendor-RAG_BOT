# PM SVANidhi - Complete Guide for Street Vendors

## What is PM SVANidhi?
PM SVANidhi (PM Street Vendor's AtmaNirbhar Nidhi) is a central government scheme launched on June 1, 2020 by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA). It provides affordable working capital loans to street vendors who were affected by COVID-19.

---

## Eligibility Criteria

### Who Can Apply:
- Street vendors in urban areas (includes hawkers, vendors, rehri-walas, thele-walas)
- Must have a Certificate of Identity (COI) issued by Urban Local Body (ULB)
- Vendors with Identity Card issued by State/UT governments or ULBs
- Mobile Vendors (those who operate from mobile carts)

### Who Cannot Apply:
- Shop owners operating from permanent shops
- Vendors in rural areas (covered under other schemes)

---

## Loan Amount - Three Tranches (Tiers)

### 1st Tranche (First Loan)
- **Loan Amount:** Up to ₹10,000
- **Tenure:** 1 year (12 months)
- **Interest Rate:** Nil (0%) - Government pays the interest as subsidy
- **Repayment:** Can be repaid in monthly installments (EMI) or bullet repayment at the end

### 2nd Tranche (Second Loan)
- **Loan Amount:** Up to ₹20,000
- **Condition:** Must repay 1st loan on time (no defaults)
- **Tenure:** 1 year (12 months)
- **Interest Rate:** Nil (0%)

### 3rd Tranche (Third Loan)
- **Loan Amount:** Up to ₹50,000
- **Condition:** Must repay 2nd loan on time (no defaults)
- **Tenure:** 1 year (12 months)
- **Interest Rate:** Nil (0%)

### Important Notes:
- Total loan amount across all 3 tranches: Up to ₹50,000
- The 3rd tranche is available only after successfully repaying the 2nd tranche
- No processing fee charged to the vendor

---

## Interest Subsidy (Cashback Incentive)
- Government provides **7% per annum** interest subsidy to banks
- This means vendors pay ZERO interest on the loan
- Additionally, vendors get **cashback of up to ₹100** on timely repayment through UPI/digital modes

---

## Documents Required

1. **Certificate of Identity (COI)** from ULB/State Government
2. **Aadhaar Card** (for KYC verification)
3. **Bank Account Details** (account number, IFSC code)
4. **Vending Certificate** or Street Vendor ID Card
5. **Mobile Number** (linked to Aadhaar for UPI payments)

---

## How to Apply

### Online Method:
1. Visit the official portal: **pmsvanidhi.mohua.gov.in**
2. Click on "Apply for Loan"
3. Enter your mobile number and verify with OTP
4. Fill in your details (Aadhaar, bank account, ULB details)
5. Upload required documents
6. Submit the application
7. Track your application status online

### Offline Method:
1. Visit your nearest bank branch (SBI, PNB, Bank of Baroda, etc.)
2. Carry all original documents
3. Fill the loan application form
4. Bank will verify your documents
5. Loan is disbursed to your bank account

---

## Repayment Process
- Repay through **UPI, PhonePe, Google Pay, or any digital mode**
- Timely repayment helps you qualify for higher loan tranches
- **Cashback of ₹100** given for each timely digital repayment
- If you miss payment, you may lose eligibility for next tranche

---

## Benefits of PM SVANidhi
1. **Zero interest loan** - Government pays the interest
2. **Digital cashback** - Earn ₹100 for each digital repayment
3. **No collateral needed** - Unsecured loan, no guarantee required
4. **Quick disbursement** - Loan credited within 7-10 days
5. **Builds credit history** - Helps you get larger loans in future
6. **Supports digital payments** - Encourages UPI usage

## FAQ - Common Questions

**Q: Can I apply if I don't have a COI?**
A: You need to first register with your local ULB to get the Certificate of Identity. Contact your municipal corporation office.

**Q: What if my loan application is rejected?**
A: You can appeal through the portal or visit your ULB office to resolve any document issues.

**Q: Can I use this loan for any purpose?**
A: Yes, it's for working capital - buying raw materials, inventory, equipment for your vending business.

**Q: Is there any processing fee?**
A: No, there is NO processing fee for PM SVANidhi loans.

**Q: What happens if I can't repay on time?**
A: Contact your bank immediately. Late repayment may affect your eligibility for the next loan tranche.
