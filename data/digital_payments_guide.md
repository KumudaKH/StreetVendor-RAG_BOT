# Digital Payments Guide for Street Vendors

## What is UPI?
UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a instant real-time payment system developed by NPCI (National Payments Corporation of India). It allows you to send and receive money directly from your bank account using a mobile app.

---

## How to Set Up UPI

### Step 1: Download UPI App
Popular UPI apps:
- **PhonePe** (most popular)
- **Google Pay (GPay)**
- **Paytm**
- **BHIM App** (government app)
- **WhatsApp Pay**

### Step 2: Register with Bank Account
1. Open the app and enter your mobile number
2. Verify with OTP sent to your phone
3. Select your bank account
4. Set your UPI PIN (6-digit PIN for transactions)

### Step 3: Create UPI ID
- Your UPI ID looks like: yourname@bankname
- Example: kumuda@paytm or vendor123@googlepay
- This is what customers use to pay you

---

## How to Receive Payment

### Method 1: Share UPI ID
- Tell customer your UPI ID (e.g., kumuda@phonepe)
- Customer opens their UPI app and enters your UPI ID
- They enter the amount and their UPI PIN
- Money reaches your bank account instantly

### Method 2: Show QR Code
1. Open your UPI app
2. Go to "My QR Code" or "Receive Money"
3. Show the QR code to customer
4. Customer scans with their phone camera
5. They enter amount and confirm
6. Done! Money received

### Method 3: Collect Payment
1. Open your UPI app
2. Enter customer's UPI ID
3. Enter the amount
4. Customer gets a notification
5. They approve the payment
6. Money received

---

## How to Send Payment

1. Open your UPI app
2. Tap "Send" or "Pay"
3. Enter recipient's UPI ID or select from contacts
4. Enter the amount
5. Add a note (optional)
6. Enter your UPI PIN
7. Payment sent instantly!

## UPI Safety Tips

### Do's:
✅ **DO** set a strong UPI PIN (don't use 1234 or birth year)
✅ **DO** keep your phone locked with password/fingerprint
✅ **DO** verify recipient details before sending money
✅ **DO** keep your bank notifications ON
✅ **DO** update your UPI app regularly

### Don'ts:
❌ **DON'T** share your UPI PIN with anyone
❌ **DON'T** click on unknown payment links
❌ **DON'T** share OTP received on your phone
❌ **DON'T** respond to calls asking for UPI details
❌ **DON'T** install apps from unknown sources

## Common UPI Issues & Solutions

### Problem: Payment Failed
**Solution:**
- Check if you have sufficient balance
- Verify UPI ID is correct
- Try again after 5 minutes
- Contact your bank if issue persists

### Problem: Money Deducted but Not Received
**Solution:**
- Money usually gets auto-reversed in 24-48 hours
- Check transaction history in your app
- Contact customer support of the app

### Problem: UPI PIN Not Working
**Solution:**
- Reset your UPI PIN using "Forgot PIN" option
- You'll need your debit card details to reset

### Problem: QR Code Not Scanning
**Solution:**
- Make sure QR code is clearly visible
- Increase phone brightness
- Clean your phone camera

## Benefits of Digital Payments for Vendors

1. **No Cash Handling** - No fear of theft or counterfeit notes
2. **Instant Settlement** - Money reaches your bank immediately
3. **Record Keeping** - All transactions are recorded automatically
4. **Build Credit History** - Digital transactions help you get loans
5. **Government Benefits** - Many schemes require digital transactions
6. **Customer Trust** - Customers prefer digital payments
7. **Cashback Offers** - Earn cashback on transactions

## Tips for Street Vendors

1. **Display your QR code prominently** at your stall/cart
2. **Keep a backup** - Use at least 2 UPI apps
3. **Send daily reminders** to yourself for reconciliation
4. **Maintain a manual register** as backup
5. **Educate customers** about your UPI payment option
6. **Start with small amounts** to build confidence
7. **Ask family members** to help if you're not comfortable with phones
