# Bengaluru Market Data & Vendor Guide

## Best Locations to Open Street Food/Vendor Shop in Bengaluru

### 1. Commercial Street (Shivajinagar)
- **Type:** Heavy foot traffic, shopping area
- **Best for:** Street food, snacks, chai, flowers
- **Rent:** ₹8,000-15,000/month for cart spot
- **Timing:** 10 AM - 10 PM
- **Crowd:** Shoppers, office workers, youth

### 2. Majestic (BMTC Bus Stand Area)
- **Type:** Transport hub, high traffic
- **Best for:** Fast food, tea, snacks, packed meals
- **Rent:** ₹10,000-20,000/month
- **Timing:** 6 AM - 11 PM
- **Crowd:** Travelers, daily commuters

### 3. Jayanagar 4th Block
- **Type:** Residential + Shopping
- **Best for:** Vegetables, fruits, flowers, daily needs
- **Rent:** ₹6,000-12,000/month
- **Timing:** 6 AM - 9 PM
- **Crowd:** Families, residents

### 4. Koramangala
- **Type:** IT hub, young crowd
- **Best for:** Modern street food, juices, snacks
- **Rent:** ₹12,000-25,000/month
- **Timing:** 8 AM - 11 PM
- **Crowd:** IT professionals, students

### 5. Whitefield
- **Type:** IT corridor, developing area
- **Best for:** Breakfast items, lunch boxes, tea
- **Rent:** ₹8,000-18,000/month
- **Timing:** 7 AM - 10 PM
- **Crowd:** IT workers, families

### 6. Yeshwanthpur Market
- **Type:** Wholesale market area
- **Best for:** Wholesale vegetables, fruits
- **Rent:** ₹5,000-10,000/month
- **Timing:** 4 AM - 2 PM
- **Crowd:** Retailers, housewives

### 7. KR Market (City Market)
- **Type:** Wholesale flower & vegetable market
- **Best for:** Flowers, vegetables, fruits
- **Rent:** ₹4,000-8,000/month
- **Timing:** 3 AM - 12 PM
- **Crowd:** Retailers, shop owners

### 8. Indiranagar
- **Type:** Upscale residential area
- **Best for:** Premium snacks, juices, organic products
- **Rent:** ₹15,000-30,000/month
- **Timing:** 7 AM - 10 PM
- **Crowd:** Affluent families, professionals

## Tips for Street Vendors

1. **Start small** - Begin with ₹5,000-10,000 investment
2. **Choose location wisely** - High foot traffic areas
3. **Maintain hygiene** - Clean setup attracts customers
4. **Use digital payments** - UPI for convenience
5. **Build relationships** - Regular customers are key
6. **Track expenses** - Maintain daily accounts
7. **Apply for PM SVANidhi** - Get ₹50,000 loan
8. **Register for Udyam** - Get MSME benefits
